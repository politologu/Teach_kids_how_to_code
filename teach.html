Resources for educators

 

W hether you're a school teacher, an administrator, an after-school teacher, or a volunteer, how2.codes provides educational resources for all ages, free of cost. Below is a summary of our curriculum and other programs. Our online courses on How to Code Studio are meant for use in all settings - in-school, home-schooling, after-school - and can be taught by teachers, parents or volunteers. If you're a parent, you can guide your child to learn online or at a local workshop or camp, or help us in other ways.

How to code

 

T he first step to introduce children to computer science is to participate in the Hour of Code, the largest learning campaign in history. In one hour, students (and teachers) can learn that computer science is fun, easy, and accessible at all ages.
